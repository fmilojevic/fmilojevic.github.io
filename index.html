About

I am a Research Specialist at the Normal Lab, University of Chicago. I was previously at LSE, where I obtained a Master's in Econometrics and Mathematical Economics (EME) and LMU Munich from where I hold a BSc in Economics.

My research interests lie at the intersection of behavioral IO, development economics, and political economy.

You can find my full CV here.

I've had the privilege of advising and mentoring several younger students over the past years. If you have any questions regarding studying Economics, the EME, being a Pre-Doc, pursuing Econ Grad School, or just want to connect, don't hesitate to reach out!
